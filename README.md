# Instant Shuttle Disembark

When a shuttle (or any mod's airship built on the vanilla shuttle, e.g. Medieval Transport's airship) lands, vanilla unloads its contents one thing at a time, roughly one per second. This drip-feeds your colonists and animals out slowly.

This patch makes a landed shuttle eject ALL of its passengers, animals and cargo at once, the instant unloading begins. It reuses the vanilla unload logic (drop priorities, drop mode, lord/quest cleanup) and simply removes the per-item throttle, so it stays compatible with quest shuttles and other shuttle mods.

Pure Harmony patch on the vanilla ShipJob_Unload; no XML, no def changes.
